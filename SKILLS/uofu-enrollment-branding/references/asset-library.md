# University of Utah Enrollment Campaign Asset Library

Use this catalog to select the correct source files for production deliverables.

## Asset Packs

## Campaign Badge

- Location: `assets/campaign-badge/`
- Primary contents:
  - `_EPS/_CMYK` and `_EPS/_RGB` (vector source for print/digital production)
  - `_PNG/` (ready-to-place badge variants)
- Use for: badge placement in social graphics, print collateral, web hero panels, and CTA sections.
- Guardrails: keep badge colors to approved campaign options and pair with university logo per brand rules.

## Patterns

- Location: `assets/patterns/`
- Primary contents:
  - `_EPS/_CMYK` and `_EPS/_RGB`
  - `_PNG/1. Light`, `_PNG/2. Medium`, `_PNG/3. Bold`
  - `_Swatch Files/ArrowPatternSwatches_*.ai`
  - `Working with Pattern Files.pdf`
- Use for: angled directional backgrounds, section separators, and layered campaign texture.
- Guardrails: apply as supporting visual rhythm; do not overpower headline and CTA readability.

## Textures

- Location: `assets/textures/`
- Primary contents:
  - `_Vector/_CMYK` and `_Vector/_RGB`
  - `_PNG/RGB_Panel_*`
  - `_Swatches/HalftonePatternSwatches_*.ai`
  - `Working with Texture Files.pdf`
- Use for: halftone overlays, depth in color fields, and rugged campaign atmosphere.
- Guardrails: preserve contrast for text; keep overlays subtle enough for accessibility.

## Icons

- Location: `assets/icons/Icons Samples.ai`
- Use for: iconography aligned to sharp-corner campaign geometry.
- Guardrails: maintain simple forms and consistent stroke/shape language across icon sets.

## Mountain Range Art

- Location: `assets/mountain-range/Mountain Range Art.ai`
- Use for: ridgeline motifs, corner cuts, and directional composition cues.
- Guardrails: retain 45-degree campaign geometry and avoid decorative overuse.

## Color Swatches

- Location: `assets/color-swatches/`
- Primary contents:
  - `ColorSwatches_RGB.ase`
  - `ColorSwatches_CMYK.ase`
- Use for: authoritative color setup in Adobe workflows.
- Cross-reference: `references/color-palette.md` for parsed human-readable values.

## Typography

- Location: `assets/typography/`
- Primary contents:
  - `rigidsquare-bold-webfont.ttf`
  - `Typography_RigidSquare.png`
  - `Typography_MyriadPro.png`
  - `Typography_Mokoko.png`
  - `Typography_MixingType.png`
- Use for: font activation, typographic comps, and visual style checks.
- Cross-reference: `references/typography-guidelines.md` for usage and fallback rules.

## Selection Rules

- Prefer RGB assets for digital and CMYK assets for print.
- Prefer vector (`.ai`/`.eps`) source files when output requires scaling or editing.
- Use PNG exports for rapid mockups or channels that do not require vector editability.
- Keep final outputs consistent with strategy, color, and typography references.
