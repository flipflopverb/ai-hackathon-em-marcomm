## Core Usage Rules

- Lead with primary palette: Utah Red, Black, White.
- Use secondary colors as accents.
- Keep target balance near 75% primary and 25% secondary where practical.
- For digital deliverables, specify HEX/RGB.
- For print deliverables, specify CMYK and require vendor proofing against official physical samples.
